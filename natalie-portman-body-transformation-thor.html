    Predictably, a set of dumbbells appears to be the major secret behind all those arm muscles. “It was really fun. I worked with a trainer, Naomi Pendergast, for, I think it was, four months before shooting, and then obviously all the way through filming,”   Portman said to Vanity Fair.
