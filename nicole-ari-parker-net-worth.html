    After graduating with an acting degree from the University of New York, Parker started appearing in several critically acclaimed films which include Boogie Nights, The Adventures of Sebastian Cole, 200 Cigarettes and The Incredibly True Adventure of Two Girls in Love.

    Her major breakthrough came in when she was offered a role at the series Soul Food. Later on, she took the lead role in the film Exiled: A Law & Order Movie.  Together with Martin Lawrence, Parker was also a cast of Welcome Home Roscoe Jenkins and Blue Streak.
