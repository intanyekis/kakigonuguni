    It's been ten years since doctor Zoe Hart showed up in Bluebell, Alabama after inheriting her late father's practice. Rachel Bilson, who won us over as Summer in The O.C., was the star of this hit CW drama. Hart Of Dixie ran for four seasons until it was unfortunately canceled in 2015. Rachel Bilson has talked about the possibility of a revival, but she and the rest of the cast have moved on to many different ventures since the show ended.

    RELATED: 15 Sweet Facts About The 'Hart Of Dixie' Cast Behind The Scenes

    Wilson Bethel, Jaime King, Scott Porter, Cress Williams, and a few others made waves since departing from Bluebell. These talented actors and actresses were part of different successful projects in the past and more recently. The whole cast of Hart Of Dixie has been successful in the acting industry and they are continuously expand their net worths. Let's take a look at these Southerners' impressive net worths!

    6 Cress Williams: $1 Million

    After being the Mayor of Bluebell for years, Cress Williams moved on to another CW series called Black Lightning. This American superhero drama series ran for four seasons with Williams as the main character. Before playing Mayor Lavon Hayes, he was in the popular television shows Prison Break and Close to Home. Williams' estimated net worth is said to be $1 million from his many television and movie appearances over the years.

    5 Scott Porter: $3 Million

    After starring in Friday Night Lights for the first three seasons, Scott Porter made his way into the land of Dixie. Porter's character, George Tucker, had romantic relations with the lead character Zoe Hart but another man stole her heart. After Hart Of Dixie was canceled, Porter appeared in The Good Wife, Scorpion, and more recently, Ginny & Georgia. Currently, Porter has a net worth of $3 million but with the new popular Netflix series Ginny & Georgia gearing up for its next season, it is highly likely he will profit even more.

    RELATED: Why Rachel Bilson And Hayden Christensen Split After 10 Years Of Marriage

    4 Jaime King: $4 Million

    Jaime King began as a fashion model who then turned her attention towards acting. Luckily this industry was made for her and she appeared in many popular movies like Bulletproof Monk, Cheaper by the Dozen 2, Pearl Harbor, White Chicks, and more recently, Escape Plan 3 and Out of Death. Taking a television role in Hart Of Dixie was the first-ever TV gig King ever did. After playing the neurotic Lemon Breeland she now plays Rose in Black Summer. Jaime King went from a very light role to a more serious character who is in the midst of a zombie apocalypse​​​​​​. King's net worth of $4 million is an attest to all the success she's had in her career.

    3 Wilson Bethel: $4 Million

    Bethel started off his career making guest appearances in the prime-time series The O.C., JAG, Cold Case, and NCIS. In 2009, Wilson Bethel played a prominent role in the CBS daytime soap opera, The Young and the Restless, as Ryder Callahan. From there he landed the role of Bluebell's resident bad boy, Wade Kinsella. Bethel's character was the main love interest for Rachel Bilson's character, Zoe. Their love story was deliciously written and the show wrapped up their relationship and tied it with a baby bow. After the series ended, Bethel starred in the Netflix original series Daredevil as Agent Benjamin “Dex” Poindexter and is currently on the legal drama, All Rise. Wilson Bethel has a net worth of $4 million and it will be interesting to see where he takes his career next!

    2 Tim Matheson: $7 Million

    Tim Matheson is an American actor, director, and producer who has a net worth of $7 million​​​​​​. His longtime career in acting started in the 60s with the TV series, Window on Main Street. Matheson's career has spanned over six decades in both television and film.

    Currently, Tim is on the Netflix series Virgin River alongside Alexandra Breckenridge, Annette O'Toole, and Martin Henderson. Luckily for Hart Of Dixie fans, he still plays a doctor in a small town in the middle of nowhere!

    1 Rachel Bilson: $12 Million

    Rachel Bilson has one of the most recognizable faces in the entertainment world today and that is all thanks to her impressive career. Bilson is best known for playing the role of Summer Roberts in the massively popular television series The O.C. Her success during the Orange County drama led to her appearing in the films The Last Kiss and Jumper. In 2011, Rachel Bilson landed the main role in the show Hart of Dixie and cemented her whopping net worth of $12 million. Bilson also has a podcast called, Welcome to the OC, Bitches!, with former castmate Melinda Clarke where they talk all things OC​​​​​​!
