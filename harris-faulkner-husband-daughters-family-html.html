    Harris Faulkner's Career: Stints At Local TV To FOX 

    Harris Faulkner's career started from freelance writing. Destined to lead as a TV presenter, she got the opportunity to make her first television appearance at KCOP-TV.

    The prominent media personality was born as Harris Kimberly Faulkner on 13 October 1965. Talking about her parents, her father, Bob Harris was a retired Lieutenant Colonel, and her mother is Shirley Harris. She lost her mother in December 2016.
