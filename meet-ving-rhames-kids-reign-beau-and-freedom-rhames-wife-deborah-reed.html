    The American cinema business is well-known for its legendary star Ving Rhames. Fans, however, are interested in learning more about Ving Rhames’ family.

    An American actor with the stage name Ving Rhames, Irving Rameses Rhames is most known for his roles in many motion pictures.

    Ving rose to fame when Quentin Tarantino cast him in the lead role of violent drug dealer Marsellus Wallace in the hugely successful movie Pulp Fiction in 1994.

    In the Mission: Impossible film series, where he co-starred with Tom Cruise as IMF Agent Luther Stickell, he is especially renowned for his supporting roles.

    He has also acted in many more movies, including Jacob’s Ladder, Dave, Striptease, Con Air, Only in America, Out of Sight, Entrapment, and many others, in addition to his well-known parts.

    Additionally, in the animated movies Lilo & Stitch, Stitch! The Movie, and Leroy & Stitch, he provided the voice of Cobra Bubbles.
