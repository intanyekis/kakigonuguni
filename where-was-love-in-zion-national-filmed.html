    Where Was Love in Zion National Filmed?

    Love in Zion National was filmed entirely in Utah. The filming locations include Zion National Park and Souther Kanab area.

    They mostly shot in Zion National Park because it is the premise used in the movie. The characters hike the trails surrounding the park to find the fourth vase.

    They find the hidden passage through the mountains.

    Lauren first meets Adam when she is lost in the mountain. They shared the view of the north of the Arizona state line.

    The Hallmark channel also shot another national park romance movie Glacier National Park Rescue. It was filmed in Fernie, British Columbia, Canada. The series continued with the theme of the previous winter movie.

    While it’s shot in winter, this new original one was created as a summer movie.

    Zion Mt. Ranch Resort

    The crew members started their journey through Zion Mt. Ranch Resort. They stayed there while enjoying its recreation, lodging, and dining.

    It is the best adventure resort on Zion National Park’s east side.

    On day 1 of shooting, the production designer Cameron Barrett mentioned they met with snow at Zion Mt. Ranch Resort in Utah.

    The art director shared the beautiful view of snowing on the sites on the Instagram page. He even conveyed the camping in Kanab, Utah, while enjoying the sunset.

    While shooting on the stunning mountains, the crew member enjoyed exploring the beautiful Emerald Pool hike and horse riding.

    Southern Kanab, Utah

    They faced challenges while shooting the summer movie in Utah due to its weather conditions.

    The crew member constantly fought against the weather while shooting. As a result, it was the best and worst experience of the crew’s lives.

    They wanted to make a summer movie, and Utah never got their request for sunshine as it showered with lovely snow almost daily.

    The Soratola LLC producer Elizabeth Soto-Lara said the group artist made the impossible possible by melting snow, hiking mountains, and riding ATVs to get the perfect shot.

    Raechele Alldridge showed the crew beautiful Utah and told them stories and legends.

    The editor Aaron Kesler added they spent 2.5 weeks in southern Utah. They were snowed upon while filming the summer movie, which was not ideal.

    However, the excruciating power of movie magic pulled through once again to work on the project.

    Overall, it was a memorable adventure for them.

    Meet The Cast Of Love in Zion National On Hallmark

    Love in Zion National A National Park Romance cast includes Cindy Busby and David Gridley. Bourke Floyd plays the supporting character.

    Cindy Busby As Lauren

    Cindy Busby played the role of Lauren. She is an assistant curator who worked at the American Collection at the Denver Museum of National History.

    When her boss Grady Hollingston thought the gift was not valuable, she found the antique vase was from the Pueblo Nation.

    As a result, she learned about the fourth vase and started her journey toward Zion Nation Park to find it and donate it to the nation.

    On the way, she met Adam, who shared an interest in her quest.

    The Montreal-born Cindy Busby is known for her arrival in Supernatural, Dairy of a Wimpy Kid, and The Big Year.

    In 1999, she debuted in the TV series Undressed as Christine. She recently played in Joy for Christmas, Marry Me in Yosemite, and Crown Prince of Christmas.

    David Gridley As Adam Proudstra

    David Gridley starred as Adam Proudstra, an American Indian park ranger at Zion National Park. He is a Puebloan.

    When he feels curious about Lauren’s quest, he agrees to aid her in discovering the fourth vase. As a result, he became her guide while she was here in the park.

    The actor is acknowledged for his appearance in The Duff, Team Kaylie, and The Last Ship.

    Recently, David Gridley played in Pretty Smart, Maggie, and Wake Up. He starred in the upcoming shows Wishper to the Thunder, Far Haven, Drift, and The Happiest Days of Our Lives.
