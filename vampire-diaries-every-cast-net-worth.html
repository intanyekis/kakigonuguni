    Once upon a time, vampires ruled the world. Well, not in the literal sense of the word, but there was a time, not that long ago, when you couldn't change the channel without being hit by some sort of movie or television series that was somehow related to vampires. Truth be told, Twilight fever opened a lot of doors for production companies to make some serious bank.

    One of the byproducts of this was, of course, The Vampire Diaries. Based on a series of books that had been around long before Edward and Bella came into our lives, the show was so popular amongst the young-adult audiences that it lasted for eight entire seasons. Even though it met its end in 2017, The Vampire Diaries was a landmark many fans still remember fondly. And with that being said, let's take a look at how much it helped the cast with their own personal wealth.

    Michael Malarkey - $1 Million

    Michael Malarkey's role as seductive vampire Enzo was a turning point in the actor's career. Even though the British-American star had his first role in 2001, he wouldn't be cast again until eight years later, in 2009, in the TV movie The Selection. 

    Related: Every Cast Member Of Outlander's Net Worth

    Malarkey kept on acting in a series of small roles, mostly in documentaries, short films, and made for television movies. Once he was cast as Enzo, the roles kept coming, and he has since been featured in projects that include Raw, Impirioso, The Oath, and, most recently, Project Blue Book. He was also featured in a Zara Larsson music video - everything comes together for a nifty $1 Million net worth!

    Matthew Davis - $2 Million

    Professor Alaric Saltzman was such a popular character throughout the run of the original Vampire Diaries, Matthew Davis was lucky enough to be brought back into the series spin-offs, The Originals and Legacies. However, it wasn't Alaric that put Davis on the map. Long before Alaric appeared on screen, Matthew was already building his career.

    He began acting almost twenty years ago, in 2000, and ever since then, he kept a steady flow of appearances on movies and television shows going for him. And hard work does pay off! Matthew Davis currently has an estimated net worth of $2 Million.

    Steven R. McQueen - $3 Million

    Elena's little brother Jeremy went through quite a bit during his time on the show. However, what can one expect from a television series like Vampire Diaries, where death and drama seem to be the most common themes? Either way, the now 31-year-old actor was no stranger to the world of acting prior to his casting as Jeremy.

    Related: Every Sopranos Cast Member's Net Worth

    Beginning in 2005, McQueen was featured on a series of popular television shows, including Chicago Fire, Chicago P.D. and Everywood. He also has a movie coming out in 2020, which surely will add some extra numbers to his already impressive $3 Million net worth.

    Kat Graham - $3 Million

    Stunning actress Kat Graham dabbled in acting for the first time in the fan-favorite movie The Parent Trap, starring Lindsay Lohan as the lovable twins in 1998. That was not the last time Graham would be featured in a Disney project - she actually appeared in a couple of episodes of Hannah Montana way back in the day.

    Both during and after her run on The Vampire Diaries, Kat participated in a number of other projects, including 17 Again and The Roommate. She voices April on Rise of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and has two more movies coming out in the near future. No secret to how she managed to get to $3 Million!

    Candice King - $4 Million

    King's interpretation of Caroline Forbes was nothing short of fantastic, and fans everywhere were thrilled to witness the character develop so beautifully throughout the course of the show. Candice King's charisma came as no surprise, considering she's been spreading it on the screen since 2007.

    Related: The 10 Richest Men On The Bachelor, Ranked

    She kept playing the character of Caroline Forbes on the spin-off The Originals, plus, she's currently filming her new movie After We Collided, the sequel to fan-fiction based movie After. Right now Candice's net worth sits at an impressive $4 Million, but there's no question this number will continue to increase.

    Zach Roerig - $4 Million

    Ohio native Zach Roerig made his way into the acting world over a decade ago. In 2004, he was featured on an episode of Law & Order, and after the fact, he continued to make appearances on smaller movies and television series, including One Life To Live and As The World Turns.

    After being cast as Matt in The Vampire Diaries, he reprised his role on the series spin-off Legacies, like his former castmate Matthew Davis. He recently finished filming a new movie and is set to star on the upcoming television show Dare Me. Props for the hard work, the $4 Million net worth is more than well-deserved!

    Michael Trevino - $4 Million

    Everybody's favorite werewolf Tyler Lockwood was a break-through role for Michael Trevino, instantly teleporting him to fame. However, that's far from being the first role the actor ever played. He currently has 27 acting credits to his name, the first one dating back to 2005 in Summerland.

    Related: The 10 Highest Paid Talk Show Hosts In America

    He was featured in a number of episodes of well-known series, including Prison Break, Without A Trace, and Cold Case. He starred in two movies after departing the show, and he's currently playing the part of Kyle Valenti in the show Roswell, New Mexico. All in all, Trevino's net worth clocks in at $4 Million.

    Paul Wesley - $6 Million

    Arguably the sweetest half of the Salvatore brother duo was Stefan. He went through his fair share of ups and downs throughout the run of the show, but he couldn't have been loved the way he was by fans if it wasn't for the fantastic portrayal given by Paul Weasley.

    Audiences were already familiarized with Wesley's face - he's been popping on and off in several projects, mostly television series. Currently, he stars in the television show Tell Me A Story and has harnessed quite the fame as a model. His reputation and hard work following the show have granted Paul Wesley the amazing net worth of $6 Million.

    Ian Somerhalder - $8 Million

    Bad boy Damon Salvatore broke the hearts of numerous fans around the world when he announced his split from co-star Nina Dobrev, and later when he married fellow actress Nikki Reed. He couldn't stay single forever! Somerhalder had already built quite the resumé with a role in television shows like Lost and Smallville.

    Related: The Net Worth Of The Cast Of Parks and Recreation

    Much like his on-screen brother, Somerhalder also scored a handful of gigs as a model. Most recently, he finished filming a new movie and stars in the show V-Wars. Thanks to all of his on-going and past projects, Ian now has a net worth of $8 Million.

    Nina Dobrev - $10 Million

    Not a single fan of the show was able to keep composure when Nina Dobrev announced she was going to depart the show. After being shot into stardom, Dobrev decided to pursue different movie roles instead of being stuck playing Elena forever. That decision paid off in the end.

    Since leaving the show, Nina scored a number of projects, most of them on the big screen. These include Flatliners, Crash Pad, Departures, and Run This Town. She now stars on the television show Fam and has two more movies coming out. Nina Dobrev has turned into a full-blown movie star, and it shows - she has a net worth of $10 Million. Impressive!
