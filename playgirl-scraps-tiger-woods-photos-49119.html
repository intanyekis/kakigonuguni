    20 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Three days after Tiger Woods received a court order stopping the possible spread of media revealing him naked, Playgirl has decided not to run the leaked photos which appear to be Woods.

    The director of marketing for the online magazine told People that they certainly did receive the rumored photos of Woods, but because they can’t verify whether or not it’s him, they’ve decided not “to go there.” Playgirl claims that the photos were “reportedly taken by camera phone by Woods himself.”

    Friday, Woods received a court order from the High Court in London to block British newspapers, magazines, and blogs from showing any nude photos or videos of him.
    According to reports, the court order “prevents the publication of private and confidential information contained in the Order…. this Order is not to be taken as any admission that any such photographs exist, and in the event they do exist they may have been fabricated, altered, manipulated and/or changed to create the false appearance and impression that they are nude photographs of our client.” —Linda Hobbs
