    Who is Suzie Ketcham?

    Suzie Ketcham is an American reality TV star.

    She is best known for starring in the VH1 series ‘Basketball Wives’ and as the ex-girlfriend of NBA center Michael Olowokandi.

    Suzie Ketcham: Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Family

    She was born on February 20, 1978, in Summit, New Jersey, USA. She is currently 45 years old and her birth sign is Pisces.

    Her father belongs from America and her mother is a Chinese-Portuguese. She has a brother but she has not revealed his name.

    Suzie holds American citizenship but her ethnicity is Caucasian.

    Education, School/College University

    Talking about her education history, she attended Pepperdine University but it is not known in which subject she majored.

    Suzie Ketcham: Professional Life, Career

    Ketcham came into the limelight after being in a relationship with NBA star Michael Olowokandi at the age of 19.

    She appeared in the VH1 reality TV series ‘Basketball Wives’ in 2010 which follows the wives, ex-wives, and girlfriends of professional basketball players.

    Similarly, she made her appearance in ‘Big Morning Buzz Live’ (2011). After Basketball Wives ended in 2013 and she transitioned into real estate as a career.

    Later, for her work, she lived in Hong Kong, L.A., before settling down in Miami.

    When Basketball Wives was revived in 2017, executive producer Shaunie O’Neal tried to get her back on the show but she declined the offer.

    Despite playing so many films and series, she has not received any awards or nominations for her performance. Hope, she will win in the future.
