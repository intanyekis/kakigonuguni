    Emmanuel Lewis, the renowned American actor, was born on March 9, 1971. He gained prominence during the 1980s for his role as the titular character in the popular sitcom “Webster.” Despite his young age of 12 when he started his leading role, Lewis swiftly became a household name, capturing the hearts of millions.

    During his stint on “Webster,” Lewis received four Young Artist Awards nominations and became the child spokesperson for Burger King Whopper, enhancing his popularity. Apart from “Webster,” he appeared in various TV shows and films, such as “The Love Boat” and “The New Adventures of Mother Goose.
