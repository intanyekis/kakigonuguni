    Kim Zolciak is apparently looking for creative ways to make money amid her ongoing divorce and financial issues.

    According to TMZ, the reality star listed a variety of her wigs on her online shop, The Biermann’s Closet, starting at $1,500. The wigs come in a variety of styles and colors, though many are long and blonde, Kim’s signature look.

    In early May, it was reported that Kim and her estranged husband Kroy Biermann owe over $1 million in back due taxes. Specifically, they owe the IRS $1.1 million in unpaid taxes as well as $15,000 to the state of Georgia.

    The news came months after their $2.5 million Georgia mansion went into foreclosure in February after defaulting on a loan they took out against the property in 2012.

    For a time, Kim was denying the foreclosure, calling it a big misunderstanding. "She is telling everyone this is a misunderstanding and is being sorted out," a source told PEOPLE at the time. "She's not moving."

    "She's showing off her house for a reason right now," a second source added. "She's showing she's still there like, 'What's the problem? She doesn't care."

    However, the financial issues appeared to have an impact on Kroy and Kim’s marriage, as the couple have since filed for divorce.

    In court documents, Kim described their relationship as “irretrievably broken with no hope of reconciliation." She also requested primary physical custody of their minor children as well as joint legal custody in addition to spousal support.

    However, Kroy has since made his own filing with the court, and is seeking full custody of their four minor children - 9-year-old twins Kaia and Kane, 10-year-old son Kash, and 11-year-old Kroy Jr. They also have two adult daughters, Brielle, 26, and Ariana, 21, who Kroy adopted after he married Kim in 2012.

    He's also made a series of allegations against his estranged wife, including that her gambling contributed to their financial fallout.

    Court documents reveal that Kroy believes his wife is displaying “trouble behavior” and has a “compulsion” with online gambling games. Moreover, he claims she has “underlying mental health issues like depression, anxiety, narcissistic personality disorder, bipolar disorder or ADHD,” which have exacerbated her gambling.

    So far, Kim has not publicly responded to Kroy’s allegations. Their divorce remains ongoing.
