    Symonne Harrison’s Facts

  • She started dancing at the age of 2 and kickstarted her acting career when she was 7 years old.
  • She had realized her love of entertaining once she performed on the dance stage when she was only 3 years old. Her passion for performing kept on growing over the years as she carried on with her dance career joining different dance competition teams and often winning many medals at dance competitions.
  • Symonne invented the product Bear On The Chair at the tender age of 6 and has held the design patent for it. It has been sold on Amazon and Learning Express stores and was also featured on a Christmas episode of the ABC sitcom Black-ish. She has been the ambassador of the National Invention League.
  • She was raised in Cleveland in Ohio. When she was 11 years old, she had an opportunity to display her talent in LA and got quickly signed with an LA talent manager after which her family relocated to Los Angeles so that she could pursue her passion for acting. She has affirmed how much she has enjoyed working on her craft, training with other actors, along with growing as an actor.
  • She has also enjoyed modeling having appeared in the Art and Beauty magazine and having won numerous photogenic awards over the years.
  • In November 2022, she launched her own beauty, cosmetics, and personal care brand called Ennomys Beauty.
  • In August 2022, she had launched her own clothing brand called Ennomys Apparel. Ennomys is basically her name ‘Symonne’ written backward.
